Isle of Berk's

Host of our Academy, a center for dragon training and research spanning the Viking World.

Berk: A Mythic Haven

Nestled amidst the rugged seas and windswept cliffs of the vast Viking World lies the fabled Isle of Berk, a place where the timeless bond between humans and dragons was forged anew. Once a remote outpost, Berk has since become a beacon of hope and unity, a testament to the enduring spirit of adventure and camaraderie.

Zen dragoN: The Heart of Berk

At the heart of Berk's transformation stands Zen dragoN, the revered academy where dragons soar and legends are born. It was here, amidst the whispers of ancient tales and the roar of dragons in flight, that the first chapter of Zen dragoN's legacy was written.

Dreaming of Harmony

In the days of yore, when dragons were feared and misunderstood, Mr. Zenith Harmonious, the founders of Zen dragoN dared to dream of a world where humans and dragons coexisted in harmony. Guided by wisdom and courage, they carved out a sanctuary upon Berk's rugged shores, a haven where dragons of every shape and size found refuge and purpose.

Berk's Radiant Aura

As the sun rises over Berk's mist-shrouded peaks, casting a golden glow upon the dragon riders who call this island home, one cannot help but feel the pulse of magic that courses through its ancient veins. For Berk is more than just a place; it is a symbol of hope, a testament to the power of friendship and the boundless potential that lies within each and every one of us.
