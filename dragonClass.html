Fire Drake
Known for its affinity with flames, the Fire Drake is a fierce and powerful dragon capable of breathing searing bursts of fire. Its scales shimmer with crimson hues, and it often dwells near volcanic regions or molten caverns.
Frost Wyrm
Adapted to icy landscapes, the Frost Wyrm commands the chill of winter with its frosty breath and razor-sharp ice crystals. Its scales gleam with a frosty sheen, and it is often found in frozen tundras or atop towering glaciers.
Storm Serpent
Master of the skies, the Storm Serpent rules over thunderstorms and tempests, wielding lightning as its weapon. With scales that crackle with electricity, it navigates through turbulent weather, reigning over skies and oceans alike.
Earth Behemoth
Massive and formidable, the Earth Behemoth is a dragon of immense strength and endurance, with scales as hard as stone. It dwells deep underground, shaping caverns and mountains with its earth-shaking movements.
Shadow Dracolich
Born from dark magic and ancient curses, the Shadow Dracolich is a sinister and malevolent creature, cloaked in darkness and capable of draining life energy. It lurks in the shadows, haunting forgotten ruins and cursed tombs.
Celestial Guardian
Radiant and majestic, the Celestial Guardian is a noble dragon imbued with divine light and celestial powers. It protects sacred realms and heavenly domains, guiding mortals with wisdom and grace, and its scales shimmer with ethereal hues.
